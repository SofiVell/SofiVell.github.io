Consultation

A mandatory stage before selecting any type of diet. It takes place online in the format of an audio/video call, lasting up to 1 hour. During the conversation, a medical history is collected (diet history, examinations, specialist appointments throughout life, current condition, pet preferences and owner's wishes). If necessary, additional examinations are prescribed and their interpretation. Answers to additional questions
